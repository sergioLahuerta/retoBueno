    FAQ

    How long will it take to receive my order?

    Our average delivery time is approximately 30 minutes. Occasionally, we may receive a large volume order before yours, which may delay your order preparation by 5-10 minutes. Our policies guarantee that the distribution of each order takes place immediately after it has been prepared, as we want to deliver you the best product, with the best quality and in the shortest possible time.

    How do you manage to keep the food hot and fresh during distribution?

    Our transport system was specially created to ensure that we keep our products hot and that they arrive at the right temperature for you to enjoy, regardless of the product.

    Can I return my order?

    We accept refund or return requests within 1 hour of receiving your order. Items must be unused and in their original condition. Please note that gift cards and perishable items are non-refundable. For assistance, contact our customer support team as soon as possible.

    Are allergens indicated on the menu?

    Yes, all allergens are clearly listed on our restaurant menu. If you have any questions or specific dietary concerns, feel free to ask any of our team members, they’ll be happy to help you make an informed choice.

    Can I customize my burger?

    Of course! You can remove, change, or add ingredients to your liking from our digital menu. We want you to have exactly what you're craving.

    Why don't you deliver to my area?

    We are expanding our service to other areas and cities as soon as possible, but we always try to ensure that we have all our operations in place to guarantee the delivery of the best service and the best products in the best conditions. For now, we are in LA but keep hungry because we will open soon in San Diego and Las Vegas too.

    Does the home delivery service cost?

    The delivery of the order may involve shipping costs or other costs which will be informed before the confirmation and payment of the order depending on the minimum amount established or other concepts. Anyways, you must know that all orders of minimum 30$ are free to delivery costs.

    What is the "Display Flex" burger and why does it have a paper wrapper around it?

    The "Display Flex" is one of our most special burgers: it's wrapped in ultra-thin edible paper that lets you eat it without making a mess. This paper keeps the ingredients in place and can be chewed along with the burger, without altering the flavor. Ideal for those who don't want to stop even to clean their hands!

    Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

    Yes, we offer 100% vegetarian and vegan burgers made with fresh and tasty ingredients. You can also customize any burger to suit your dietary needs.

    Can I place an order from my mobile phone?

    Yes. You can order from our website or from our official website, available on iOS and Android. Your burger is just a few clicks away!
